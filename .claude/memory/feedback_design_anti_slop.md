David strongly dislikes "vibe coded look" and "AI slop design." Specifically called out:
- The highlighted/accent border treatment on insight cards (the green/amber left-border highlight look)
- Generic dashboard aesthetics that look like every AI-generated template
- Redundant information displayed in multiple places (e.g., docs checklist shown twice)
- Decorative elements that don't serve a purpose

**Why:** The product targets professional tax preparers (Antonio). It needs to look intentional, trustworthy, and polished — not like a hackathon demo or vibe-coded prototype.

**How to apply:** Intelligence cards should differentiate through content, not decorative borders. Design should be subtle and tasteful. Reference the ShareWillow warm aesthetic from the v2 redesign. When in doubt, simpler is better.
